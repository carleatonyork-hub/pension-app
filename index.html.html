Our Methodology
Cleava Ethical Check
How we decide
PASS or FAIL
We believe letter grades like C+ create false comfort. A fund with 56% harmful exposure doesn't deserve a passing grade. So we don't give one.
The Cleava Standard
FAIL
The fund holds 0.5% or more in revenue from any sector you have chosen to exclude.
PASS
The fund holds less than 0.5% in revenue from every sector you have chosen to exclude.
Why 0.5%?
The 0.5% de minimis is the standard threshold used across the ethical fund industry for exclusion-based screening. It accounts for the practical reality that index funds and multi-asset funds may hold trace positions through tracking drift or indirect exposure.

Any fund with holdings genuinely trying to exclude a sector will sit below 0.5%. Any fund that isn't trying will be well above it — often by a factor of 10 or more, as with the Aviva Default Growth Fund.
What we actually check
📊
Data source
Morningstar Direct — fund-level holdings data including underlying company revenue by business activity, updated quarterly.
🔍
Revenue-based screening
We check what percentage of each underlying company's revenue comes from excluded activities — not just whether the company name sounds harmful.
📋
Your exclusions, your check
The PASS/FAIL result is personal to you. A fund may PASS for one user and FAIL for another depending on which sectors they've chosen to exclude.
What this check does NOT do
✕ Rate investment quality, expected returns, or risk
✕ Constitute regulated financial advice of any kind
✕ Guarantee future ethical compliance — holdings change quarterly
✕ Replace independent financial advice if you are unsure whether a transfer is right for you
Limitations
Holdings data from Morningstar is typically 30–90 days delayed. New positions acquired by a fund after the last disclosure date will not yet be reflected in our check. We display the data vintage date alongside every result so you know how current it is.

If you believe a result is incorrect, you can flag it in the app and our team will manually review within 5 working days.
